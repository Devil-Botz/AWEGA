Treasure Hunt

Venue: Seminar hall

Team Size: Max 5 Members | Fee: ₹100/head

  • Registration: Each member must register individually.
  • Reporting: 8:30 AM at registration counter.
  • Rounds Breakdown:
    • Round 1: Quiz (20 min)
      10 GK, 5 Logic (Math twisted), 5 Interstellar Theme.
      2 marks per right answer. Printed paper provided.
    • Round 2: Task (30 min)
      Perform given tasks. Team must stay together and reach allotted place on time. No personal belongings allowed.
    • Round 3: Puzzle (15-20 min)
      3 puzzles per team. Teams qualifying within time limit move to next round.
    • Round 4: Final Hunt (1-1.5 Hours)
      Solve clues to find the treasure. Coordinators allocated per team. Team must stay together.
  • Conduct: Malpractice/Cheating leads to disqualification. Damages must be compensated.
Reporting Time 8:30 AM

3's Football

Venue: College Ground

Team: 4 Players (3 + 1 Sub) | Fee: ₹100/head
